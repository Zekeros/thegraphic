The Graphic

The Graphic has established itself, over the past 15 years, as the most popular free community magazine in the areas it services.

The magazines carry local stories, information and advertising from local businesses.

There are currently three issues of the Graphic Magazine delivered monthly door to door. Each magazine is limited to a maximum of 5000 homes allowing us to keep the content local and interesting for the communities they serve.

The Graphic Print Shop is located in The Wheatsheaf Courtyard in the centre of Market Bosworth. Having opened 13 years ago it is the home of the Graphic Magazine and also offers a Design and Print Service.

Our work includes:
  • digital print,
  • litho print,
  • signage,
  • banners,
  • wide format poster/plan printing,
  • promotional goods and much more.
